Splits source image of dimension WxH into four rectanglar regions: r1: [0, 0]~[x, y]; r2: [x+1, 0]~[W, y]; r3: [0, y+1]~[x, y+1]; r4: [x+1, y+1]~[W, H]; then reassembles from [r1, r2; r3, r4] to [r4, r3; r1, r2]. Offsets x, y are determined using polar representation: x=dist*cosine(deg), y=dist*sin(deg). Modular if x/y. Then the `moved' image is weight-added to original image greater than original dimension.
